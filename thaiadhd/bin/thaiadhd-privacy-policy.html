2. What the Application can be used for

The purpose of the FULL ADHD mobile Application is to encourage users to maintain their own health and that of their loved ones. The App is designed to help users identify ADHD, its symptoms, as well as the risk factors, and to support users in obtaining additional information about the treatment of ADHD. The mobile Application is not intended to diagnose a disease and it does not replace the consultation of a doctor. The App is intended for people aged 16 and over.
